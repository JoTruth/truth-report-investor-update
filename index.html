4. Key Milestones

Major achievements during the December 2025 – February 2026 reporting period.

🎤
Future of Nations Conference (FNC) — 25 February 2026
The FNC was a landmark success. Fully funded with sponsorships paid, the event generated close to 50 high-quality content pieces in a single day — dramatically lowering average cost per video and filling the Q2 content pipeline. The "Suing the ANC" piece from the event became the top-performing post of the period with 666,918 impressions.
💳
Netcash Payment Integration — Live
Netcash is fully integrated and tested. The platform can now accept user donations via a two-click experience (Apple Pay / Google Pay). This establishes the technical foundation for user wallet balances and future subscription transactions.
📊
Community Polls — Live
Community Polls launched successfully on the platform. Frictionless single-click engagement. This is the first strategic step toward the future introduction of prediction markets — a key premium product differentiator.
🎙️
Truth Report Live — Weekly Anchor Show
Truth Report Live is established as the weekly anchor show on Tuesdays, focused on South African politics and current affairs. The premiere episode registered 15,000 views within the first 24 hours and generated R500 in Superchats.
🏆
Podcaster Search — Completed
The Truth Podcaster Search initiative concluded with a live finale featuring the top 10 candidates. Winners have been selected and are producing content for Truth Report, funded via Grassroots Mzansi NPC.
📰
MyBroadband Feature
Truth Report was featured in MyBroadband as a new online news platform competing with News24 and IOL, highlighting backing from Rob Hersov and Nick French and positioning the platform as a "well-funded, big podcaster."
🌿
Grassroots Mzansi NPC Integration
The NPC is fully operational as a "sidecar" funding and donations vehicle with Section 18A certification, enabling tax-deductible donations for qualifying contributors.
📉
57.8% Reduction in Monthly Burn
Operating spend reduced from R642,645 (August 2025 peak) to R271,180 in January 2026. This was achieved through the transition to remote operations, software subscription optimisation, and people cost management.
